Installation Instructions

  1. Download and unzip the extension file to a location on your computer
  2. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/
  3. Enable "Developer mode" by toggling the switch in the top-right corner
  4. Click "Load unpacked" button and select the unzipped folder
  5. The extension will appear in your browser with the shield icon
  6. A setup page will automatically open to help you get started

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add websites to block?

You can add websites to block by clicking on the extension icon in your browser toolbar, selecting "Options", and then entering the domain names in the input field. Click "Add Website" to add each site to your block list.

Can I block websites temporarily?

Yes, Website Blocker is designed for temporary blocking. You can set a custom duration (in minutes) for how long you want the websites to be blocked. After the specified time has passed, the blocking will automatically deactivate.

What happens when I try to visit a blocked website?

When you try to visit a blocked website, you'll see a blocking page with a "Website Blocked" message and image. After a brief moment (3 seconds), the tab will automatically close, helping you stay focused on your tasks.

Can I use wildcards to block multiple subdomains?

Yes, you can use wildcards (e.g., *.example.com) to block all subdomains of a particular website. This is useful for blocking entire platforms with multiple subdomains.

Is this extension free to use?

Yes, Website Blocker is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees or premium features.

How do I uninstall the extension?

To uninstall the extension, go to chrome://extensions/, find Website Blocker in the list, and click the "Remove" button. You can also right-click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar and select "Remove from Chrome".
